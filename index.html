Features

Customized Souvenirs and Giveaways: Make Your Event Unforgettable!

Customized Bags

We offer a range of styles, including tote bags, Drawstring pouches & backpack, zip pouch, coin purse, laundry bags, and many more. Each bag can be printed with your event logo, message, or design to make a lasting impression on your guests.

Custom Apparel

We also offer a range of other custom apparel items, including t-shirts, caps, hoodies, and more. Each product can be personalized with your own design or message, making them perfect for teams, events, and promotional giveaways.

Other Personalized products

At Jeesprint, we're not limited to bags and apparel - we also offer a wide range of customizable products to meet all your needs. Whether you're looking for mugs, pillow keychains, foldable fans, umbrellas, pens, USBs, tumblers, or anything in between, we've got you covered with a huge selection of high-quality products that can be personalized to match your unique style.

How It Works

Easy Steps to Get Your Custom Products from Jeesprint.

Choose Your Product & Select or Upload Your design

Browse our website to find the product that you want to customize, such as bags, apparel, or other products like mugs, pens, or umbrellas.

Decide on the design, logo, or message that you want to print on your product. You can provide us with your own design or work with our designers to create a new one.

Preview Design - Edit if Needed - Place Order

Get a real-time preview of your customized product to ensure it meets your expectations.

Make any necessary adjustments until you're completely satisfied with the final design.

Add your personalized products to the cart and proceed through our secure checkout. Choose from multiple payment options.

Production - Packaging - Track and Enjoy!

Our skilled team uses top-notch printing techniques and quality materials to craft your customized souvenirs.

Your items are carefully packed to ensure they arrive in perfect condition.

Monitor your order's progress and estimated delivery date. Once they arrive, unbox and share the joy of your personalized products.
